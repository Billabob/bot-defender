Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

The extension doesn't work, what am I supposed to do?

There are a variety of things that could be causing the extension not to run. First make sure you have a subscription, then restart the extension from chrome://extensions. It may take a few minutes to register your subscription.

If that doesn't solve the problem, click on the extension's icon to bring up the page with an on-off switch (that you had to click to open this info page). If the switch is bright red and says "OFF", click it to turn on Bot Defender. If it's still not working, join our Discord server and tell us what's wrong.

Can't I just set my trades to followers only?

Yes, this will block most bots. But it also reduces the amount of legitimate trades you receive, thanks to the added effort it takes to send you a trade. Many users will stop bothering if they see that your trades are closed, my experiments showed that I receive around 20% less trades than when they're completely open (excluding bots of course). You'll also be affected by this as you'll have to follow users every single time you send a trade.

I'm on the list but I'm not a bot?

Appeals can be made in our Discord server.

What information can this extension access?

As can be seen in our code, which is accessible to all, the extension itself does not send us information of any kind. It simply declines trades from certain users who are flagged as bots. The only data we have regarding the extension is the amount of people who downloaded this extension, provided by Google, and the revenue we get from the VIP server subscription.

I'm being spammed by a bot but the extension isn't declining their trades, why?

We have a manually compiled list of known bots due to it being near-impossible to detect them automatically. This means we will be missing some new botters, so if you want someone added to the list please join the Discord server and show us screenshots of their trades.

Why does this cost 350 Robux per month?

I'm a small scale developer and I need money to pay the bills. The Robux can be Devexed - it all helps, my financial situation isn't very stable, and 350 robux isn't a steep price to pay in comparison to the sheer pain of declining 200 botted trades every morning - it's the same price as an egg.
